About Me

Hi, I am a first year Astrophysics student at the University of Cambridge, working with Eloy de Lera Acedo in the Cavendish Radio Cosmology group. My work involves analysing the 21cm hydrogen line at high redshifts in order to observe what was happening during the cosmic dawn.
