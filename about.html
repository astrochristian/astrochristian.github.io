About Me

Hi, I am a third year Astrophysics student at the University of Cambridge, working with Eloy de Lera Acedo and Dominic Anstey in the Cavendish Radio Cosmology group. My work involves using Bayesian analysis to detect the global 21cm hydrogen signal at high redshifts with the REACH radio telescope in order to constrain our knowledge about the Epoch of Reionisation and Cosmic Dawn.
